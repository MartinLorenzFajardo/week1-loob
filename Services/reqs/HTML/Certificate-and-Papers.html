BARANGAY CERTIFICATE

This confirms the residency of an individual within the barangay.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fee
  • Community Tac Certificate (Cedula)

BARANGAY CLEARANCE

Verifies that you live in a particular barangay. It shows your good moral character that you haven't caused any trouble in the community and have no pending legal cases.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • 75 pesos Fee
  • Community Tac Certificate (Cedula)

BARANGAY CLEARANCE

Also known as a Barangay Business Clearance, It certifies that the business is allowed to operate within its jurisdiction.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fee
  • Community Tac Certificate (Cedula)

BARANGAY CERTIFICATE OF INDIGENCY

A Certificate of Indigency is issued by the MSWD OFFICE certifying that the said client belongs to an indigent family in their barangay as certified by the Barangay Captain.

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Fee
  • Community Tac Certificate (Cedula)
